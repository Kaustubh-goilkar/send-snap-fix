We Are SnapFix

At SnapFix, we believe in the power of everyday citizens to make their communities better — one photo at a time. Our platform makes it easy for anyone to report problems like potholes, broken lights, or sanitation issues.

Just snap a photo, submit a few details, and we’ll make sure your report reaches the right place. Our admin team reviews and updates the status so you can track progress until the issue is resolved.

Snap it. Submit it. Fix it. That’s SnapFix.
