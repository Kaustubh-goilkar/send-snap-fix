About Us

SnapFix empowers you to improve your community by simply taking a photo. See something wrong? Snap it, submit it — we’ll help get it fixed.

We Are SnapFix

At SnapFix, we believe in harnessing the power of the public to identify and solve problems in real time. Our platform allows anyone to report issues like broken streetlights, potholes, or sanitation concerns in seconds.

Once submitted, our admin team reviews the report, updates its status, and works to ensure a timely resolution. You can easily track the progress — from submission to solution.

It's fast. It's simple. It's SnapFix.
